How I Work

My Process

A structured approach to ensure every project delivers exceptional results, on time and on budget.

01

Let's Talk

We discuss what you need - your goals, ideas, and timeline. I make sure I understand exactly what you want before we start.

  • Requirements gathering
  • Target audience analysis
  • Competitor research
  • Project scope definition
02

Design

I put together a design that matches your vision. You review it, tell me what to change, and we refine until you're happy.

  • Wireframe creation
  • Visual design mockups
  • Brand integration
  • Revisions (up to 2 rounds)
03

Build

I code your website with clean, efficient code. You'll see progress along the way - no surprises, just steady work toward your finished site.

  • Responsive development
  • Clean code practices
  • Weekly progress updates
  • Performance optimization
04

Launch

We test everything, fix any issues, then your site goes live. I'll help you get it online and show you how to make simple updates yourself.

  • Cross-browser testing
  • Performance testing
  • SEO setup
  • Post-launch support
